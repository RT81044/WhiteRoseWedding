About Us



At White Rose Wedding, we're not just wedding planners; we're dream weavers. With a passion for turning your special day into an extraordinary adventure, we specialize in crafting weddings that are as unique as your love story.

Whether you dream of saying "I do" on a cruise, in the sky on an airplane, or amid the romance of a train journey, our dedicated team is here to make your wedding dreams come true.
